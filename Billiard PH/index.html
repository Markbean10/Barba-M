BILLIARD PH

Billiards is a precision sport that encompasses a variety of games played on a rectangular table with pockets. Originating in the 15th century, it has evolved into several popular variants, including pool, snooker, and carom. Each game requires a combination of skill, strategy, and mental acuity, as players aim to control the cue ball and pocket their target balls. Billiards is not only a test of physical dexterity but also a social activity, often enjoyed in pubs, clubs, and dedicated billiard halls. Its rich history and global appeal make it a timeless pastime, attracting players of all ages and skill levels.
